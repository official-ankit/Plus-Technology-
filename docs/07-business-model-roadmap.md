# Computer Institute System — Business Model & Roadmap

## Pricing Models

### Model Options

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Freemium** | Free courses + paid certs/premium | Volume, brand awareness |
| **Subscription** | Monthly/annual access | Retention, predictable revenue |
| **Per-Course** | One-time purchase per course | High-value courses |
| **Bundles** | Track bundles at discount | Upselling, commitment |
| **B2B** | Enterprise licenses | Large contracts, stability |

### Recommended: Hybrid Freemium + Subscription

#### Free Tier
- Access to introductory modules (first 2 modules per track)
- Limited code runner executions (20/day)
- Community forum access
- No certificates

#### Pro Subscription ($19/month or $149/year)
- Full access to all courses
- Unlimited code runner
- Certificates included
- Priority support
- Mentor Q&A access

#### Certification Exams
- $49 per certification exam
- Retakes: $25
- Pro subscribers: 1 free exam/quarter

#### Enterprise (Contact Sales)
- Volume licensing (min 10 seats)
- Admin dashboard & reporting
- SSO integration
- Custom content options
- Dedicated account manager

### Pricing Table

| Item | Free | Pro Monthly | Pro Annual | Enterprise |
|------|------|-------------|------------|------------|
| Price | $0 | $19/mo | $149/yr ($12.4/mo) | Custom |
| Courses | Limited | All | All | All + Custom |

| Code Runs | 20/day | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Low course completion** | High | High | Gamification, reminders, mentorship |
| **High churn** | Medium | High | Improve onboarding, engagement features |
| **Content quality issues** | Medium | Medium | Review process, student feedback loops |
| **Technical scalability** | Low | High | Cloud-native architecture, load testing |
| **Security breach** | Low | Critical | Security audits, encryption, monitoring |
| **Competition** | High | Medium | Differentiation via certs, simplicity |
| **Instructor shortage** | Medium | Medium | Competitive pay, community building |
| **Regulatory changes** | Low | Medium | Legal monitoring, flexible policies |
